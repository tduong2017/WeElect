Your Vote Matters

Voting is your right as a citizen, but with so much unknown context surrounding your options - it is easy to feel lost as a voter. Why make decisions on such foggy circumstances? The time is ripe now for you to understand your political atmosphere without difficulty

The App

  • Easy

    Plug in your zipcode and you are well on your way

  • Convenient

    This can be done from a few taps on your phone, nothing more

  • Transparent

    And for your efforts, everything you need to know about your representatives will be revealed

It is business done easy

Your time, money, and knowledge is limited - yet managable. When voting, you'll want to know who is trying to represent you and, most importantly, how the candidate will do it.
It's business, and you want the best deal. With this app, and your zip code, you will learn who is currently in an elected position of power, what they vote for, and what they have done for you via legislation.
Your power to make informed decision increases, and it begins simply within you, your zip code, and this app.
